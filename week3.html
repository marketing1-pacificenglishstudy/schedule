Activity Descriptions ｜ 活動說明摘要

  • Boomerang Art Activity ｜ 迴力鏢藝術創作活動： Design and decorate your own boomerang while learning about Aboriginal art. 學習原住民藝術元素並親手創作專屬迴力鏢作品。
  • Beach & Surf Workshop ｜ 沙灘與衝浪安全工作坊： Learn lifesaving skills and beach safety while enjoying the surf. 認識海灘安全與救生觀念，並參與沙灘與浪上活動。
  • Animal Welfare Program ｜ 動物照護體驗課程： Learn how to care for different animals and support their wellbeing. 透過餵食與互動了解動物需求與保育重要性。
  • Team Building Activity 3 ｜ 團隊建立活動 3： Collaborative tasks that build communication and problem‑solving. 透過第三階段團隊挑戰，強化合作與解難能力。
  • Shopping Experience ｜ 購物體驗： Explore Australia Fair and look for souvenirs. 在 Australia Fair 逛街購物，體驗當地生活與文化。
  • Wildlife Sanctuary ｜ 野生動物保護區： Get close to Australian wildlife such as kangaroos and koalas. 近距離接觸袋鼠、無尾熊等動物，了解生態保育。
